The goal behind sales should have nothing to do with the act of being as persuasive as possible to sell a product. If anything, this is a big no-no. Rather, a salesman will want to \textbf{guide. }

To guide someone, you need to have expertise, so it is important to note that your industry, company, and product knowledge must be deeper than your prospect. Specifically, you must understand the product you are selling, the business you are operating within, your prospect's business, and the market itself.
