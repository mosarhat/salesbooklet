\section{Learning Outcomes}

A common issue in sales is the loss of a potential close. This usually stems from a failure to act, rather than the product. The number one marker of success is confidence. You cannot have zero self-perception, you must be confident in your skills, and fearless in this industry.
To solve this, there needs to be a delusional form of self-belief in your skills and abilities as a salesperson.
There are 3 steps to being professional:
1.	Understanding the value you bring to the table. 
2.	Behaving like a professional. Be curious, and respectful. 
3.	Present your solutions in a professional, caring manner.
By being professional, you have EARNED the right to ask for the buyer’s business. Asking for the order is not an imposition. 
To ask for an order, you need to be authentic. Trying to be someone you will not lead nowhere. People can smell bullshit. Be empathetic.
